Things to Do in Bengaluru

1. Bangalore Palace

A palace inspired by Windsor Castle in England, with distinct Tudor style.

2. Ulsoor Lake

Located in the heart of the city, Ulsoor Lake is one of the largest lakes in Bangalore spanning an area of over 120 acres.

3. Government Museum

Established in 1865, the Government Museum is one of the oldest museums in India.

4. Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace

The summer abode of the former ruler of Mysore, Tipu Sultan.

5. ISKCON Temple

Perched on Hare Krishna Hill, this is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna.

6. KR Market

One of the largest flower markets in Asia.

7. Bull Temple

A sixteenth-century Hindu temple dedicated to Nandi, the bull.

8. Visvesvaraya Industrial and Technological Museum

A science and technology museum, located just metres away from the Government Museum.

9. Janapada Loka

A folk museum where you’ll learn about Bangalore’s fascinating history.
